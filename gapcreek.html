Gap Creek Reserve

An iconic attraction for all mountain bikers and hikers across Brisbane. With professionally built walking and mountain biking trails.

The developement of the trails is constantly maintined by Brisbane City Council and has been in operation for a number of years.

Having attracted several tourists to its nearby landmark known as 'Mt Cootha', the trails become extremely popular over the weekends, both early in the morning and as the sun is setting.

The green space is peaceful and calming where walks, sitting and listening to the nature rustle in the trees is likened by people of all ages.
