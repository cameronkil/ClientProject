Brookfield general store

Developed in the late 1800s

The meet up place most mornings of the week. Where laughter and conversation is exchanged over coffee.

Also has room for functions and celebrations to be held from small to large groups.

Is also a pinnacle for cyclists in the area to meet for coffee after a ride as it is located within the notorious cycle route called 'The Three Fingers'.

Has been constantly repainted and developed for the past couple of decades.

Overall there is a calming, peaceful and chill vibe that the store gives off that comes from the history and time that it has been in operation. Everyone appreciates it and there is never negativity expressed toward the Brookfield General Store. HIGHLY RECOMMEND!

Key Historical dates

Built nearly 150 years ago!

1871

This particular building was erected as a teacher's residence, along with the school building.

1932

The residence served the Education Dept until c1932 when representatives of the Trustees of the Brookfield Recreation and Show Ground Reserve purchased it in 1932. It was subsequently leased as a store to which has served the Brookfield community.

1988

Libby Wager in her work Historic Brookfield (1988), in discussing the store, stated that "Brookfield Store remains a focal point of the community".
