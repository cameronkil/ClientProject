About the website

Designed with the intent to showcase some of Brookfields interesting history as well as what there is to do. Although being located 30 minute drive from Brisbane CBD there is still plenty of small but fun activities to take part in, even if it is purely socialising at a cafe or celebrating in a almost two-century old community hall.


The general vibe and happiness of the people in Brookfield is an attraction in itself. With minimal noise and only a few shops, the peace that comes from the natural space and wild animals makes the suburb feel safe and refreshing.


To be able to display some cool aspects of Brookfield for the public to view could definitely develop some interest in coming to visit. Due to the vibe of the people in Brookfield... You're welcome anytime!
